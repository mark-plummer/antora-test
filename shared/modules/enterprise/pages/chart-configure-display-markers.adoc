= Show data markers
:last_updated: 2/24/2020
:permalink: /:collection/:path.html
:page-partial:
:summary: You can show data markers for a line chart.

For some charts, like line charts, you can show data markers.

To show data markers, follow these steps:

image::shared:enterprise:chart-config-data-markers.gif[Show data markers]

. While viewing your search or answer as a chart, click the *chart configuration* icon image:/images/icon-gear-10px.png[] on the top right.
. Click *Settings* at the bottom of the *Customize* menu.
. Select *Data Markers*.
